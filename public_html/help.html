Hi! Welcome to the Help Page.

If you haven't already made an account with us, click the "Sign Up Here" message to get started. Once you have made an account, sign in. You will have 20 minutes before your session is terminated. Your login is protected through a salt and hash function!

Once you are on the home page, click on the "Search By Major" tab. On this page, you can sort through a list of majors using a keyword, or by scrolling through the list. After selecting a major you are interested in, you can read reviews left by students who are in these programs at various colleges. If you are looking for a particular university, try filtering for a specific college. You can also filter high to low or low to high of the likes and dislikes.

On the account page, you can see reviews, comments, likes, and dislikes that you have made.

We hope you enjoy the site!
